yamaha Motor Company

Yamaha motor Co. Ltd is a Japanese mobality manufucture that produces motorcycle,motorboats,outboard motors,and other motorised products. The company was established in 1955 upon separetion from nippon Gakki co.,Ltd. And is headquartered in Iwata,Shizuoka,Japan.

In motorcycle racing Yamaha has won 39 world championships,including seven in motoGP and 10 in the preceding 500 cc two-stroke class,and two in world superbike
